Chicken Tenders

Description

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 Tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup or honey
  • 2 cups cornflakes
    • 1 1/2 lb boneless, skinless chicken tenderloins
  • Cooking spray
  1. Preheat oven to 400Â°. Line a large baking sheet with parchment. In a shallow bowl, whisk flour, 1 tablespoon onion powder, and 1 teaspoon paprika; season with salt and pepper. In another shallow bowl, beat eggs to blend with syrup. In a third shallow bowl, toss cornflakes with remaining 1 tablespoon onion powder and 1 teaspoon paprika; season with salt and pepper.
  2. Dredge chicken in flour mixture, shaking off excess. Dip into egg, letting excess drip off, then coat in cornflakes, lightly pressing to adhere. Transfer chicken to prepared sheet, then spray with cooking spray.
  3. Bake chicken until cooked through and coating is crispy and golden brown, about 25 minutes.
